One of my favorite places I've been was the Narrows in Zion National Park. Last summer I booked the permits to hike the Narrows. The first half of the trip, we stopped by Great Basin National Park and took a tour of Lehman caves and checked out the bristlecone pine trees. Once we entered Utah. We stopped by Bryce Canyon National Park. The hoodoos were cool. After that, we headed down to Zion and met up with the rest of our group. We headed out early in the morning and hiked over half way. Then we camped in the Narrows and then hiked out the next day. It was stunning. I highly recommend hiking the Narrows if you have the chance.

Check out the Narrows on the National Park Service Website! https://www.nps.gov
